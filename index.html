Megalodon Shark facts

1.The word Megalodon shark comes from Greek meaning "great tooth".

2.Megalodon was the largest fish that ever lived. The largest individuals measured up to 58.7 feet or even larger.

3. It's thought that megalodon ate 2,500 pounds of food per day.

4.Most fish are exclusively cold-blooded, but megalodon may have been like today's great white sharks, which are considered partially warm-blooded because of the heat they generate while swimming. This would have allowed megalodon to hunt in colder waters.

5.Female megalodons may have been about twice as large as the males. Even an infant megalodon was huge, at least 6.6 feet from nose to tail.
